THE CHEF SERENDIB

The chef Serendib will be Sri Lanka’s biggest cooking competition for the year 2021/2022 which will be held at selected venues on the second week of January 2022. This is the first ever cooking competition organized by a Rotaract club in Sri Lanka which is initiated by the professional development avenue of the Rotaract club of informatics institute of technology, with the aim of encouraging and empowering individuals who have chosen the food industry as their career path while also preventing them from leaving the food industry.

03 STAGES OF THE COMPETITION

1st Round

150 participants competing for 40 minutes to create a chicken dish worthy of carrying them into the second round.

2nd Round

20 lucky contestants cooking authentic Sri Lankan food using firewood.

3rd Round

The final 5 prepares a 3-course meal within 4 hours, with their eye on the price.

Some Common Questions Were often asked

Is there any registration fee to compete in the competition ?

Yes, registration fee is Rs.500

Will everyone be competing in teams or individually?

All contestants will compete individually.

What kind of qualifications are requirements to participate in the competition?

All contestants should be able to cook atleast a simple chicken dish.

Is there an age limit for competitors ?

Yes, only participants between the age of 18-30 can participate.

What happens if we cant complete cooking within the allocated time limit?

The contestant will be disqualified and wont be able to participate in the next round.

Will adequate ingredients be provided to all cooks?

Yes, adequate ingredients and equipments will be given to all contestants.
